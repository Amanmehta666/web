About PB11 Transport

Professional Freight & Logistics Services Across Australia

Who We Are

PB11 Transport Pty Ltd was established in 2019 and has grown into a trusted freight and logistics provider delivering reliable transport services across Australia.

Headquartered in Dubbo, NSW, we specialise in freight transport, distribution, and supply chain solutions tailored to businesses of all sizes.

Our operations are built on safety, compliance, efficiency, and long-term client partnerships. We are committed to maintaining the highest industry standards while delivering dependable logistics solutions nationwide.
